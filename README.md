We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

- **User Content:**

- **Subscription Information:** If you purchase a subscription, we collect payment information through our third-party payment processors (Apple App Store, Google Play Store, and Adapty).

- **Communication Data:** Any messages, feedback, or correspondence you send to us.

### Information Collected Automatically

- **Device Information:** We may collect information about your device, including device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.

- **Usage Data:** We collect information about how you interact with our App, including features used, time spent, and actions taken.

- **Analytics Data:** We may use analytics services (such as Firebase Analytics) to collect information about App usage, crashes, and performance.

### Information from Third-Party Services

- **Adapty:** We use Adapty to manage subscriptions and in-app purchases. Adapty may collect information about your subscription status and purchase history.

- **OpenAI:** When you use AI features, your content is sent to OpenAI for processing. We do not store your content on OpenAI's servers beyond what is necessary for processing.

When you use AI features (scenario generation, profile review, coaching), your content is sent to OpenAI for processing. This includes:
