Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upgrade to Premium?
Tap the Premium icon in the app, or go to any premium feature and tap "Upgrade to Premium". You can choose between monthly or yearly subscription plans.
How do I restore my purchases?
Go to the Paywall screen and tap "Restore Purchases". This will restore your Premium subscription if you've purchased it on another device or after reinstalling the app.
Why can't I access premium features?
Premium features require an active subscription. If you've purchased Premium but can't access features, try restoring purchases or contact support for assistance.
How do I delete my account?
Contact us at ashobal@sinfosecurity.com with your request. We'll process your account deletion and remove all associated data within 30 days.
How is my data protected?
We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS) for all data transmission. Your data is processed securely and we never sell your personal information. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
I found a bug or issue
Please email us at ashobal@sinfosecurity.com with details about the issue, including your device model and iOS version. We'll investigate and fix it as soon as possible.
